What We Study
Research Areas
🧠
Post-Stroke Epilepsy
Predicting and preventing epilepsy after stroke using EEG biomarkers, multicenter cohort data, and the SeLECT scoring system.
SELECT Consortium · EEG
⏱️
Late-Onset Epilepsy
Understanding new-onset epilepsy in adults over 55 — its links to neurodegeneration, dementia, and the neuroprotective potential of antiseizure medications.
TriNetX · UK Biobank
💊
Drug Outcomes & Pharmacotherapy
Real-world effectiveness and safety of antiseizure medications, immunomodulators, and novel agents across large federated healthcare databases.
TriNetX · Pharmacoepidemiology
🧬
Biomarkers & EEG
Identifying blood-based and electrophysiological biomarkers of epileptogenesis — including NfL, GFAP, extracellular vesicles, and quantitative EEG features.
IMPOSE · FINESSE
⏳
Brain Aging & Neurodegeneration
Studying accelerated biological aging in epilepsy using PhenoAge, structural MRI brain age, and longitudinal population cohorts.
UK Biobank · ENIGMA
🤖
Clinical AI & NLP
Applying large language models and automated pipelines to extract structured data from EEG reports and clinical records at scale.
MedGemma · Local LLMs
